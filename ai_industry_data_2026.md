## 5. Key Technology Trends (2025-2026)

### A. AI Agents
- 2025 widely called "the year of AI agents"
- OpenAI launched "Operator" agent (Jan 2025)
- Anthropic launched "Computer Use" (late 2024) - agents controlling desktops
- Google launched Project Mariner and Agent Space
- Microsoft Copilot agents integrated across Office/365
- Salesforce Agentforce for enterprise workflows
- Market projection: AI agent market expected to reach $47B+ by 2030

### B. Reasoning Models
- OpenAI o1 (Sept 2024): chain-of-thought reasoning
- OpenAI o3/o3-mini (late 2024/early 2025)
- DeepSeek R1 (Jan 2025): open-source reasoning, major impact
- Reasoning models showed dramatic improvements on math/science benchmarks
- Trend: reasoning + agentic capabilities converging

### C. Multimodal Models
- GPT-4o: native multimodal (text, image, audio, video)
- Gemini 1.5/2.0: 1M+ token context, multimodal
- Claude 3.5: strong vision capabilities
- Video generation: Sora (OpenAI), Runway Gen-3, Kling
- Native audio: real-time voice conversations (ChatGPT Advanced Voice)

### D. Local/On-Device Models
- Apple Intelligence: on-device models for iPhone/Mac (2024)
- Google Gemini Nano: on-device for Android
- Meta Llama 3.x: widely deployed locally (8B, 70B, 405B)
- Mistral, Qwen, Phi models: strong local performance
- Ollama, LM Studio: popular local inference tools
- Trend: smaller specialized models outperforming larger general ones

### E. MCP Protocol (Model Context Protocol)
- Introduced by Anthropic (Nov 2024)
- Open standard for connecting AI models to external tools/data
- Rapid adoption: OpenAI, Google, Microsoft all adopted MCP by early 2025
- Compared to "USB-C for AI" - universal connector standard
- Enables agent-tool interoperability across different AI providers
- Ecosystem growing rapidly with community-built MCP servers
