## Protocol & Standards

### Model Context Protocol (MCP)
- **Introduced**: Anthropic (November 2024)
- **Adoption**: OpenAI, Google, Microsoft all adopted by early 2025
- **Purpose**: Universal standard for AI-tool integration
- **Analogy**: "USB-C for AI" - universal connector
- **Impact**: Enables agent interoperability across providers

### Open Standards Movement
- **Interoperability**: Push for AI systems to work together
- **Tool Integration**: Standardized function calling
- **Data Formats**: Common formats for AI inputs/outputs
- **Safety Standards**: Global push for AI safety protocols
